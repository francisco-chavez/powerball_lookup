# powerball_lookup
I was just wondering what the best numbers to play would be.

The Powerball isn't uniformally random. They use physical objects that contain imperfections. Because of this, some numbers will show up more often. Because of this, some numbers will show up less often. So, this raises the question of, which numbers to pick, and which numbers to avoid. On top of that, there's also a question of whether or not nowing this changes the chances enough to risk playing. This will look through the results of past Powerball results in a few different ways and combine that with the costs and possible winnings just to see what we come up with.
